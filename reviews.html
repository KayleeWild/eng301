Highest Reviews

The collection of community housing options on this website comes from the list available on the university's website. To ensure quality and relevance, it excludes any that are located outside of Rexburg or complexes that don't have a website. It is not a comprehensive list.

Quality Matters

While cost and location are important, they mean a lot less if you can’t stand to live in the apartment. This page is dedicated to the best quality options for married housing in Rexburg. They are all rated on Google maps with an average of 4 stars or higher. Enjoy superior options that make you rest easy.

415 Flats

$930 - $955

19 min Walk

4.9 Stars

The best-rated community housing in town! With a near 5-star average, the reviews of 415 Flats speak for its high quality. It’s often referred to as one of the best complexes in Rexburg! Other comments frequently praise the management for their friendly manner and prompt response to problems. With the spaciousness and cleanliness that this complex offers, you can’t go wrong.

Cedars Heights

$950 - $1,095

11 min Walk

4.7 Stars

Sitting high atop Hemming Village, you won’t miss out on any fun living at Cedars Heights. The prime location overlooks several shops and food places on 2nd South, with Porter Park just down the street and the university a block away. It’s the newest married housing complex and they strive for affordable quality. The reviews rave about the in-apartment washer and dryer, fabulous view, and covered parking! The friendly maintenance team is a big plus too. You’ll have everything you need at Cedars Heights.
