Why Does Location Matter?

Not everyone has a car, and even those that do will save money by not driving to school every day. All the options below are at most a 15 minute walk away from the MC roundabout. A little walk is a great way to start off your day and will get you some fresh Idaho air without freezing your face off or wearing out your legs too much. Being close to the campus has other benefits too. Rexburg is literally built around the university, so all the major shops, food places and entertainment are within a few blocks as well. Not to mention the temple sits just across the street from the south end of campus. Living closer to the university will make life easier in more ways than one!

Liberty Corner

$775 - $945

6 min Walk

4.1 Stars

The average distance from campus for married housing is a 21-minute walk. Liberty Corner is just across the street! It sits conveniently adjacent to the Hart gym, which means it will take you just as much time to get to the STC as it will to reach Paramount 5. Hemming Village is only a 4-minute walk down the street, and with the lower rent, you can justify the urge to grab a treat from Kiwi Loco or any of the other food places on 2nd South. Talk about convenience!

Campus View

$945

7 min Walk

4.4 Stars

Surprise! Campus View is owned by the same management as Liberty Corner (it’s also referred to as Liberty Corner II). That means that here you’ll enjoy all the same benefits as Liberty Corner, just adding 1 minute to your walk! Pricing is the same, but Campus View doesn’t offer the 1-bedroom option. The reviews, however, are higher here than Liberty Corner. Between these two options you’ll surely find what you’re looking for.
