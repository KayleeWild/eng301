About this site:

Pricing. Amenities. Proximity to campus. Utilities. Furnishings. Pet-policies. Ratings. How do you keep track of it all?! As a soon-to-be-married student here at BYU-Idaho, I’ve seen how tedious it is to get a vision of what you’re working with. Luckily, I’ve looked through dozens of housing options so that YOU can look no further! ‘Perfect Apartment’ organizes Rexburg married housing options into categories you care about so that you can find the perfect place to live with your spouse.

The collection of community housing options on this website comes from the list available on the university's website. To ensure quality and relevance, it excludes any that are located outside of Rexburg or complexes don't have a website. It is not a comprehensive list.

For more community housing options visit

the university's community housing page.
